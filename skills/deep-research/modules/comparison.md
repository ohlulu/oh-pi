# Comparison Search Strategy

## When to Use
Query asks to compare tools, frameworks, services, or contains "vs", "alternative", "which is better", "compare".

## Target Sites
- Benchmark and review sites with methodology disclosure
- reddit.com — community discussions with real-world experience
- news.ycombinator.com — technical community opinions
- Independent developer blogs with hands-on comparisons
- Official comparison pages from vendors (read with bias awareness)

## Query Patterns
- "{A} vs {B} benchmark 2025"
- "{A} vs {B} reddit"
- "{A} vs {B} comparison site:news.ycombinator.com"
- "{A} alternative 2025"
- "{A} vs {B} performance latency throughput"
- "{A} migration from {B} experience"

## Source Evaluation
- **Prefer**: benchmarks with disclosed methodology, migration case studies, community threads with multiple perspectives
- **Deprioritize**: vendor marketing pages (biased), listicles without depth, AI-generated comparison articles
- **Red flags**: "Top 10 Best" SEO articles, affiliate-driven reviews, comparisons without version numbers or dates
