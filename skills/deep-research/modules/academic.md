# Academic Search Strategy

## When to Use
Query mentions papers, research, ML, science, studies, experiments, or academic concepts.

## Target Sites
- arxiv.org — preprints, ML/AI/physics/math papers
- scholar.google.com — cross-discipline academic search
- semanticscholar.org — AI-powered paper discovery
- acm.org / ieee.org — CS conference proceedings
- biorxiv.org / medrxiv.org — biology/medical preprints
- openreview.net — peer review and conference papers

## Query Patterns
- "{topic} site:arxiv.org"
- "{topic} survey paper 2025"
- "{topic} benchmark results"
- "{topic} site:semanticscholar.org"
- "{topic} conference paper ICML NeurIPS ICLR 2025"

## Source Evaluation
- **Prefer**: peer-reviewed papers, official proceedings, established research groups
- **Deprioritize**: blog summaries of papers, AI-generated explainers, pop-science articles
- **Red flags**: papers with no citations and no known authors, predatory journal domains, content farms rewriting abstracts
